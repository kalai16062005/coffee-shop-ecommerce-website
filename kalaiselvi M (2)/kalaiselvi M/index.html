🔍 About

📜 History

Coffee was first discovered in Ethiopia in the 9th century. Local legends speak of a goat herder noticing the beans’ energy. Arab traders soon embraced the drink, brewing it with tradition. From there, it traveled to Europe and became a global favorite.

🎯 Mission

We strive to deliver only the finest, ethically sourced coffee. Each cup reflects care, quality, and sustainability. Our space is crafted for comfort and connection. Every guest is welcomed with warmth and respect.

👁️ Vision

Our vision is to be a coffee brand cherished worldwide. We aim to bring people together through every cup. Sustainability lies at the heart of our mission. With every sip, we hope to uplift and inspire.

🤝 Team

Our team is built on passion, skill, and a love for coffee. From baristas to roasters, every member brings dedication. We strive for excellence in every cup we craft. Sustainability is the heart of everything we do.
